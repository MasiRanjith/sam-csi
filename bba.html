Bachelor of Business Administration - BBA

The B.B.A course provides a well-rounded curriculum that blends practical,

real-world application and theoretical aspects of business in a global context. Students are encouraged to reach their highest potential through collaborative relationships that motivate and sustain growth individually and organizationally.

Special Features

Students are encouraged to realize their potential through collaborative relationships that motivate and sustain growth individually and organizationally.

Internship

Major project work for three months and institutional training in a manufacturing unit for two weeks is to be completed by students.

  Financial Accounting

  Production and Material Management
